SilverStore for Developers

Register with your email, pay a one-time fee, and get approved to upload apps or digital assets to the public store.

Developer Registration

Pay a one-time fee of 300 KSH and contact the owner to become an approved developer.

Once approved, you can upload apps, templates, and digital assets to the public store.

Owner / Admin Upload

As the owner, sign in with your admin email to upload apps you have built and publish them directly.

Admin uploads are auto-approved, while developer uploads require approval.

Public App Gallery

Visitors can browse approved apps anytime without signing in, just like a Play Store experience.
